About Hawlader Express

Hawlader Express is a dynamic and diversified service provider dedicated to enhancing financial and digital accessibility across Bangladesh. With a strong presence in rural and semi-urban areas, we aim to empower communities by delivering essential services right at their doorstep.
Our core operations span across agent banking and internet connectivity, serving as a critical link between established institutions and the underserved population. Through strategic partnerships with leading banks such as Dutch-Bangla Bank PLC and NRB Bank PLC, we provide a wide range of agent banking services—including account opening, cash deposit and withdrawal, utility bill payments, remittance disbursement, and savings schemes—all facilitated via biometric authentication. These services are designed to promote financial inclusion, especially for those without access to traditional banking infrastructure.
In addition, through our Nexusnetonline venture, we offer reliable and high-speed broadband internet services to homes, businesses, and institutions. Our focus on uninterrupted connectivity and responsive support ensures that customers stay connected in an increasingly digital world.
At Hawlader Express, we are committed to service excellence, technological adaptation, and community impact. Whether it’s enabling a rural farmer to access their bank account or empowering a student with fast internet for online learning, we are proud to be a trusted partner in progress.

Dutch Bangla Bank Agent

In collaboration with Dutch-Bangla Bank PLC, we offer comprehensive agent banking services. This includes customer registration, cash deposits and withdrawals, ATM services, balance inquiries, statement requests, bill payments, person-to-person fund transfers, and biometric-based savings schemes like DPS and FDR. Our aim is to provide secure and convenient banking solutions to underserved populations.
